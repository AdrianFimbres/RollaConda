Core Mechanics

Master the Fundamentals

Utilize a unique control scheme to execute takedowns, passes, and submissions. Manage your stamina and outmaneuver your opponent in a high-stakes battle of wits.

The Path to Victory

Win by submitting your opponent or by scoring the most points for dominant positions. Illegal moves will result in penalties, so fight with honor... or don't.

Choose Your Grappler

Select from a roster of unique fighters, each with their own special moves and grappling styles. Will you be a pressure passer, a leg-lock specialist, or a submission artist?

Global Combat

Compete in a variety of futuristic arenas from around the world, each with its own dynamic lighting and electrifying atmosphere.
